## Bio Sketch

I am a graduate student in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at Texas Tech University. I study accretion properties of Active Galactic Nuclei through spectral, timing and polarimetry observations. My particular interest is in the origin of X-ray coronae, spectral states of the accretion disk, and wind properties of AGN.
I did my undergraduate at Ashoka University with a major in Physics and a minor in Astronomy, where I was introduced to the field of high energy astrophysics while working on temporal and spectral properties of stellar mass black hole binaries.
